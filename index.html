COMMON QUESTIONS.

What exactly is a static site?
Static sites are pre-rendered websites that don't rely on complex databases to load. This means they are incredibly fast, more secure, and cheaper to host than traditional WordPress sites.
How long does the process take?
I specialize in high-speed delivery. Most projects are completed within 10 to 14 days, from intake form submission to final deployment..
Will I be able to edit the content myself?
Yes. I integrate Headless CMS solutions that allow you to update text and images as easily as writing an email, without any risk of breaking the site's code.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. I also offer maintenance packages for business owners who want a completely hands-off digital presence.
